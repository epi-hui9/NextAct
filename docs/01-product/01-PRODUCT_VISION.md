# Product Vision

## North Star

Client Instrument is a completely private space that belongs to each executive. It helps turn forty years of judgment into a visible living legacy, and a difficult professional low point into the beginning of a hopeful next chapter.

## Intended user

A 50–70 year old executive who may be unfamiliar with technology. One obvious path for every important action.

## Experience principles

Private. Calm. Deliberate. Premium. Coherent. Warm. Trustworthy.

## Current scope

The Production Trust Loop only: install, vault, onboarding, Home→Talk, persistence, tree growth, daily reminder opt-in.

## Explicit future direction

Strategy, Brand, Marketing generation, Firm OS, GraphRAG, native wrappers, and social features remain out of scope until the Trust Loop is proven with paying pilots.
