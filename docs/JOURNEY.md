# Nine month path

The Path tab shows the eight-stage journey:

1. Tell Your Story
2. See Yourself Clearly
3. Set Your Target
4. Reconnect Your People
5. Build Your AI Edge
6. Get Seen
7. Your Daily Edge
8. Land It and Keep It

Only **Tell Your Story** is active today. Its completion percentage is the deterministic Story progress from `lib/story`. Later stages remain visible and locked.

The living tree (Seed through Living canopy) is a separate emotional visual. It never shows a percentage next to the stage name.
